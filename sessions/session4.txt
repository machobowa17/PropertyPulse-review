--- HEADLINE METRICS FIX ---

- avg_price, median_price, price_per_sqft headline numbers now computed via
  raw PERCENTILE_CONT / AVG on core_property_transactions (rolling 12 months)
  — NOT the old approach of AVG(monthly medians) from core_property_prices_lsoa
- Old approach was wrong because AVG(monthly median) ≠ median of all transactions
  Example: Manchester true median = £240,000 vs old "median" = £267,000 (12.7% gap hidden)
- Fix only affects LAD/county searches with large transaction counts
  (For single postcode/LSOA with 1 transaction, median = average = correct either way)
